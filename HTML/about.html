The Vision: Avlory, Empowerment, and Unity

My ultimate goal is to empower people, built on the conviction that information is a human right. Through Avlory, I pledge to dedicate my team's efforts into building that dream, starting with our financial application, Penny Sphere.

Penny Sphere is designed to bring in social features and allow users to not only track their own expenses but also manage group budgets for shared expenses or track their kids' spending. More importantly, it provides vital financial literacy on practices such as credit building and savings accounts.

Our goal is to build software and hardware that truly elevates technology as a tool for the user. Our transparent, privacy-focused model puts control in the user's hands and helps build our community, as division of people is the greatest opponent humanity faces.
